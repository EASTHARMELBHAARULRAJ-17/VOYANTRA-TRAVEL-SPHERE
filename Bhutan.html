About Bhutan

Bhutan, known as the Land of the Thunder Dragon, is famous for its peaceful monasteries, breathtaking Himalayan landscapes, and rich Buddhist culture. The country offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, spirituality, and adventure. Visitors can explore ancient fortresses, scenic valleys, and traditional villages while experiencing the warm hospitality of the Bhutanese people.

Top Attractions

  • Tiger's Nest Monastery - Cliffside Monastery
  • Punakha Dzong - Historic Fortress
  • Himalayan Valley - Scenic Mountain Views

Best Time To Visit

  • March To May
  • September To November
